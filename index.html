Who We Are

Your presence will be welcomed when we gather for worship at 8:00 am on Sunday, Every Week.

We will engage in songs of praise, prayer, sharing of the Lord’s Supper, an opportunity for a free will offering, and a sermon from Caleb Cochran on War Against the Darkness 5: “Gods” and Demons. The Scripture Reading and context will be Deuteronomy 32:15-17


We will meet again for prayers at 9:00 pm. As the last week night of every month, we will primarily worship through singing. You will have the opportunity to share in the Lord’s Supper should you so desire.


We also gather for Bible Study every Monday night at 6:00 pm and Wednesday night at 7:30.








Location & Meeting Times

1450 West Sunyani Avenue Kwatire , MD 20850


Sunday 7 am · 11:30 am · 6 pm | Wednesday 7:30 pm



From the Rockville Metro Station, you can take Bus Route 54 and get off at either Hurley Ave. or Research Blvd. The building is about 1/4 mile from either of those stops.
